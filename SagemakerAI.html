What is AWS SageMaker?

Imagine you want to open the greatest restaurant in the world. You need to prep ingredients, invent recipes, cook the food, serve it fast, and make sure it always tastes good.

AWS SageMaker is that giant, automated Master Kitchen, but for Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). It gives you every single tool you need to take raw data (ingredients), train an AI model (cook the recipe), and let computers around the world use it (serve the food).
